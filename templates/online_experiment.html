Welcome to Our Online Experiment!

Thank you for your interest in participating in our study. The experiment consists of three parts:

1. Training

  • The training section will guide you through the task step by step using animations.
  • Feel free to run the training as many times as you like until you are comfortable with the task.

2. Practice Trials

  • In this section, you will practice trials similar to those in the main experiment.
  • After each response, the correct answer will be displayed. Please use this opportunity to improve your understanding of the task.
  • Your responses in this section will not be saved. You may practice as many times as you wish.
